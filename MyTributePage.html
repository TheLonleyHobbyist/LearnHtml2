Aristotle Socrates Onassis; 20 January 1906 - 15 March 1975, [1] commonly called Ari or Aristo Onassis, was a Greek-Argentine shipping magnate, who amasseed the world's larges privately owned shipping fleet and was one of the world's riches and most famous men.
